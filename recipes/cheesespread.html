Pimento Cheese Spread

Who wants boring crackers? Not me. In this guide I'm gonna teach you to make some of the best cheese spread you've ever had. Its not only great for crackers but tortilla chips, sandwiches, it even tastes great on toast. Its simple and delicious, and its easy to make Let's get started.

Ingredients

    1 and a half punds of shredded American cheese
    2 cups of mayonaise
    4 oz of diced pimientos
    12 a cup of white sugar

Steps

    Stir shredded cheese, mayonnaise, pimentos, and sugar together in a bowl.
