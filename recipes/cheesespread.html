Cheese Spread

Thanksgiving Stuffing

Who wants boring crackers? Not me. In this guide I'm gonna teach you how to make the best cheese spread you've ever had (according to allrecipes.com). Its not only great for crackers but tortilla chips, sandwiches, and even toast. Its simple and delicious. All right enough of the intro, let's get started.

Ingredients

    1 and a half pounds of shredded American cheese
    2 cups of mayonnaise
    4 oz of diced pimientos
    12 a cup of white sugar

Steps

    1. Stir shredded cheese, mayonnaise, pimentos, and sugar together in a bowl. That's it you're done! Enjoy!
